# AI-Marketing-Lab-Generative-AI-Recommendation
# Generative AI and Recommendation Engine

## Overview

This project demonstrates two important AI applications in marketing:

- Generative AI for content creation  
- Recommendation Engine for product suggestions  

These techniques help businesses automate marketing and improve customer engagement.

---

## Problem Statement

Businesses need to:
- Create large amounts of marketing content quickly  
- Recommend relevant products to customers  

Manual processes are slow and inefficient.

This project uses AI to automate content creation and provide product recommendations.

---

## Objectives

- Generate marketing content using AI  
- Build a recommendation system using product ratings  
- Understand AI applications in business  

---

## Technologies Used

- Python  
- Google Colab  
- Pandas  

---

## Part A: Generative AI

### Description

Generative AI is used to create marketing content such as:

- Product descriptions  
- Promotional emails  
- Ad copy  

### Example Use Cases

- Writing product descriptions  
- Creating email campaigns  
- Generating ad taglines  

### Business Impact

- Saves time  
- Reduces manual effort  
- Improves content quality  

---

## Part B: Recommendation Engine

### Method

- Dataset created with user ratings  
- Average rating calculated for each product  
- Top-rated products recommended  

### Output

Top recommended products based on ratings.

### Business Impact

- Improves customer experience  
- Increases sales  
- Supports personalized marketing  

---

## Results

- AI-generated marketing content  
- Product recommendations based on ratings  

---

## Limitations

- Small dataset used  
- Recommendation is not personalized  
- Generative AI output depends on prompts  

---

## Future Enhancements

- Use advanced recommendation models  
- Integrate real-world datasets  
- Build full AI marketing system  

---

## Conclusion

This project shows how AI can automate marketing tasks and improve business decision-making.
